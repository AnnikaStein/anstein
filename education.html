Talented pupils who attend a BEGYS class complete the intermediate level (grades 7 to 10) in only three instead of four years (you formally skip grade 9). In principle, the same curriculum applies to BEGYS classes as to regular classes. BEGYS is aimed at students who are particularly motivated, capable and willing to perform. A recommendation conference suggests a pupil for inclusion in BEGYS if a successful completion can be expected with regard to performance, work posture and overall mental state.
