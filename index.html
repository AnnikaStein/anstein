Welcome!

I am a doctoral student in experimental particle physics, working on the Compact Muon Solenoid (CMS) experiment. Currently, my research is targeted at developing deep learning algorithms for jet tagging. The tools under investigation play a crucial role when studying the properties of the Higgs boson.
