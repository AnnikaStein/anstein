## Speedsolving

???+ info

    I am a speedcuber, which means that I solve twisty puzzles against the clock - competing against others and myself. For example, it takes me around ten seconds to solve the well-known Rubik's Cube or three seconds to solve a puzzle called "Skewb". Solving puzzles and learning new strategies play an important role, but there are various related activities. For example, I'm an experienced content creator, and even before uploading on youtube, I started producing tutorials for gutefrage.net over a decade ago. Some more recent videos can be found in the box below, showing my currently favourite puzzles in action.

??? tip "Volunteer work"

    <a href="https://www.worldcubeassociation.org/persons/2014STEI03" target="_blank">Member</a> of the World Cube Association (WCA).
    Served in various roles, e.g. as Community and Staff Representative for the WCA Advisory Council, Delegate, (Lead) Organizer, Translator, Open Source Contributor.

??? tip "Competitive speedcuber"

    <p>
      Competed in 16 countries, including the World Championship 2025 (Seattle), European Championships 2018 (Madrid) and 2022 (Copenhagen).
    </p>
    <p>
      40 official podiums (5x gold, 15x silver, 20x bronze) out of 104 tournaments, obtained in categories "3x3x3", "Skewb", "4x4x4", "6x6x6" and "7x7x7".
      Female National Record holder in category "Skewb", and first place at German Online Cubing 2020, the "official" replacement of the National Championship in an online-format during the global pandemic.
    </p>
    <p>
      Usually, I combine the competitive aspect with creation of tools that assist with the training. An example is the development of open-source training software to practice solution strategies in the speedcubing category Skewb, called "SkewbSkills" (<a href="https://github.com/AnnikaStein/SkewbSkills/releases/" target="_blank">GUI application</a> with Python plus
      <a href="https://annikastein.github.io/SkewbPage/SkewbSkills/skskweb.html" target="_blank">Web solution</a> with HTML, CSS, JS).
    </p>
