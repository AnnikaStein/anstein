    #### "Begabtenförderung am Gymnasium mit Verkürzung der Schulzeit (BEGYS)"
    ##### August 2010 - July 2013

    ??? tip "More info..."

        Excerpt from [the school's homepage](https://www.nikolaus-von-kues-gymnasium.de/unterricht/besondere-angebote/begabtenfoerderung-begys.html){target="_blank"} translated to English:

        > Talented pupils who attend a BEGYS class complete the intermediate level (grades 7 to 10) in only three instead of four years (you formally skip grade 9).
        In principle, the same curriculum applies to BEGYS classes as to regular classes.
        However, the material is adapted to the shortened cycle with a smaller proportion of exercises and redistributed accordingly over the years.
        BEGYS is aimed at students who are particularly motivated, capable and willing to perform.
        A recommendation conference suggests a pupil for inclusion in BEGYS if a successful completion can be expected with regard to performance, work posture and overall mental state.
